Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our platform and services.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with the platform, including the following categories:

  • Information you provide directly
    • Account registration details such as name, email address, and profile preferences
    • Information submitted through forms, settings, or support communications
    • Any other information you voluntarily choose to provide
  • User content
    • Media, files, or data you upload, store, or manage through the platform
    • Metadata associated with uploaded content, such as filenames or timestamps
    • Content organization preferences and usage context
  • Technical and usage information
    • Device, browser, and operating system information
    • Log data, access times, and interaction patterns
    • Information necessary to maintain security, performance, and reliability

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the platform in a secure, reliable, and user-focused manner. Information is used for the following purposes:

  • Platform operation and functionality
    • Creating and managing user accounts and profiles
    • Providing access to features, tools, and stored content
    • Processing requests and actions initiated by users
  • Content management and delivery
    • Storing, organizing, and retrieving user-uploaded content
    • Ensuring content is delivered accurately and securely
    • Maintaining continuity of access across sessions and devices
  • Security, integrity, and compliance
    • Protecting against unauthorized access, misuse, or abuse
    • Monitoring system activity to maintain platform stability
    • Complying with applicable legal and operational requirements
  • Communications and support
    • Responding to inquiries, requests, or support issues
    • Sending important service-related notices or updates
    • Informing users about changes that affect their use of the platform

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only when necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, or protect users and the service.

  • Service providers
    • Third-party providers that support infrastructure, hosting, storage, or security
    • Access is limited to what is necessary to perform their services
    • Providers are required to protect information and use it only as instructed
  • Legal and regulatory requirements
    • When required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process
    • In response to lawful requests from public authorities
  • Protection of rights and safety
    • To enforce policies, terms, or agreements
    • To protect the security, rights, or safety of users, third parties, or the platform

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Technical safeguards
    • Use of secure infrastructure, access controls, and encryption where appropriate
    • Monitoring systems for vulnerabilities, threats, and abnormal activity
    • Regular maintenance and updates to support platform security
  • Operational and administrative measures
    • Limiting access to information to authorized personnel only
    • Internal policies governing data handling and system access
    • Ongoing review of security practices and procedures
  • Risk awareness
    • No system or method of transmission is completely secure
    • We continuously evaluate and improve safeguards as technology evolves

Your Choices

You have control over how your information is used and managed on the platform. Subject to applicable requirements, you may exercise the following choices:

  • Account information
    • Access, review, and update certain account details through your settings
    • Correct inaccuracies in information you have provided
  • Content management
    • Manage, organize, or remove content you upload to the platform
    • Control how your content is stored and accessed within your account
  • Account status
    • Request deactivation or closure of your account, subject to verification
    • Retention of certain information may be required by law or operational needs

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal obligations, or operational practices.

  • Policy updates
    • Revisions may be made to clarify existing practices or introduce new disclosures
    • Updates are intended to maintain transparency and accuracy
