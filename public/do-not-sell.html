Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Your privacy rights

We do not sell or share personal information for advertising or marketing purposes. This page explains your rights and how to submit a request, if applicable, under relevant privacy laws.

Do Not Sell or Share

Certain privacy laws provide consumers with the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. Although we do not sell or share personal data, we provide this page to explain your rights and our practices.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

Where required by applicable law, we recognize and honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals sent through supported web browsers as a request to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.

How to Submit a Privacy Request

You may submit a request related to your personal information by contacting us via email. Please include sufficient information to allow us to verify your request and respond appropriately.

Email: privacy@yourdomain.com

Authorized Agents

Where permitted by law, you may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. We may require verification of the agent’s authority and your identity before processing the request.

Children and Minors

We do not knowingly sell or share personal information of children or minors. We do not engage in targeted or behavioral advertising directed at children.
